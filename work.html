Black Jack Game


Launch Black Jack Game
  • Welcome to the ultimate blackjack experience, my dynamic web app crafted using HTML; CSS and JavaScript. The game strts with two random cards dealt to you. Achieve the ultimate victory with a perfect 21 known as blackjack. Strategize your way through the game by deciding wether to push for a new card. But be aware that exceeding 21 you are automatically out of the game. Test your luck in this thrilling rendition of blackjack.

  • #How I built it: Set up the two initial cards to be thwron randomly, used conditional rendering to display different messages according to the sum of the cards. Make possible to throw a new card in case the sum of both cards is less than or not equal to 21. Stop a new card to be thwron whenever the sum of both cards is more than 21. Ensure the sum is adding up whenever the new card is pushed.
