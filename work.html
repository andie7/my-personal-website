Black Jack Game


Launch Demo
  • #What it does: Welcome to the ultimate blackjack experience. Achieve the ultimate victory with a perfect 21 known as blackjack. Strategize your way through the game by deciding whether to push for a new card. But be aware that exceeding 21 you are automatically out of the game. Test your luck in this thrilling rendition of blackjack.

  • #How I built it: I began by setting up the initial two cards to de dealt randomly. Using conditional rendering I displayed different messages based on the sum of the cards. I add the functionality to throw a new card in case the sum of both cards was less than 21. Additionally I integrated a logic to prevent a new card from being thrwon if the sum exceeds 21. I ensured that the sum dynamically updated with each new card thrown.
